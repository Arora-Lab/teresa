> **Teresa Family Charity Design Philosophy**
>
> The Teresa website should evoke peace, compassion, dignity, and trust. The primary brand color is **green**, symbolizing life, rice fields, hope, and renewal. Every component should feel cohesive, modern, and understated. The interface should avoid trendy or flashy effects in favor of timeless elegance, with generous whitespace, consistent typography, and a calm visual hierarchy. Every page should feel like it belongs to the same thoughtfully designed nonprofit platform. 
> 
> Overall Feeling: Apple + National Geographic + Catholic charity (clean, warm, peaceful, trustworthy, elegant, timeless).

## Color Palette

### Primary: Primary Green (`#0B6E4F`)
Used for: Logo, Buttons, Links, Active navigation, Icons, Section accents.

### Secondary: Light Green (`#DFF6EC`)
Used for: Cards, Quote boxes, Information panels, Background highlights.

### Accent: Warm Gold (`#C8A951`)
Only for: Donate button, Important statistics, Highlights, Donation call-to-action. (Symbolizes generosity without feeling like e-commerce).

### Neutral
- **Dark**: `#1F2937`
- **Gray**: `#6B7280`
- **Light**: `#F8FAFC`
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

- **English**: `Inter`
- **Vietnamese**: `Noto Serif` (or `Times New Roman`)

## UI Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Dark Green background, White text, `rounded-xl` (`12px`).
- **Secondary**: White background, Green border, Green text.
- **Danger**: Red (Only inside Admin).

### Cards
- Simple and clean.
- `rounded-2xl` (`16px`).
- Soft shadow.
- Very subtle border.
- Lots of whitespace.
- *No gradients everywhere.*

### Hero Imagery
- Use beautiful Vietnamese imagery (Rice terraces, Sunrise, Green mountains, Elderly people smiling, Volunteers).
- *Avoid generic stock-office photos.*

### Animation
- **Very subtle**: Fade in, Slide up, Hover lift.
- *Avoid*: Spinning icons, bouncing buttons, flashy effects. (This is a charity, not a tech startup).
